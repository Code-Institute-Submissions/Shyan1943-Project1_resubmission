About Us

WM Group was established on 1998 and we run a diversified business through our subsidiaries to meet a specific set of customer needs.

With over 20 office locations across Hong Kong and China, we have a strong team of over 600 staff, working diligently to bring out the best to our clients in the industry.

Since its inception, we have grown into a group of multi¬modal transportation companies, covering Air, Sea, and Land freight modes with services ranging from common feeder, international freight forwarding, logistics and supply chain, water front and freight terminal, container and cargo trucking to liner agency.

Apart from being a One-stop logistics & Distribution services, we also work as a carrier to service the Intra-Asia market using our own containers globally. Our markets include Japan, Korea, Taiwan, USA, South America, Europe, ASEAN countries and Indian Subcontinent.


Quality Service . Right Attitude . Think Forward


In order to extend its regional reach and presence in the market, we are currently expanding our Shipping Agency branding to the whole of Asia, including India and the Middle East, to support the overall group activity and to satisfy the needs of our valuable clients. Regardless of the cargo size, we help to reduce your transportation cost and delivery time to ensure that all cargoes reach its destination safely and on time.
